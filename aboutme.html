About Me

I am Jamie Briggs, aged 22, and also a games programmer. I have graduated from Northumbria University with a first class degree in Computer Games Programming (BSc) and currently studying a masters in Advanced Computer Science (MSc).

Originally from Derby, I have live in Newcastle Upon Tyne and have for the last 12 years. I play guitar and piano in my spare time and see what I can create with the two. I currently work in NHSBSA and also an Explorer Scout Leader.

Found on this site is work I have done either in my studies in Computer Games Programming and Advanced Computer Science, or just in my spare time. I hope the projects found within this site will be of interest to whomever stumbles upon my work.
